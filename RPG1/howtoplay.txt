Welcome to Monk! In this game, your primary objective is to find the
trasure room located deep within the maze!

You may encounter goblins on your journey, so watch out! Equip yourself
with weapons and refill your health by finding pickups
throughout your exploration.

Careful! Don't get slain by a goblin, otherwise it's game over! 

Press any key to return to the main menu...
